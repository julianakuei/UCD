    Solve the problem of uneven distribution

    Introduction


    According to previous surveys and news reports, Taiwanese are willing to help underprivileged groups and the victims of disaster by donating money and supplies to charities.
    However, there is a long-standing issue that the distribution of donation is always uneven. The famous charities are easier to receive donation because of their reputation, contrastingly, the small-sized organization who also devote to doing acts of charity seems hard to get enough supply as lacking of information to acquire publics’ trust.
    Our goal is to establish a reciprocal and trustworthy platform, making people could donate without doubt and solve uneven distribution problems of donation resource.

    What we want to solve...

    To people who are willing to donate-

    Even though understanding some small-sized charities may need donation, they still incline to donate to famous charities, because they have no ideas to distinguish which charity is trustworthy.

    To small sized charities-

    Lack of fame, information and propaganda channel, so it’s hard to compete with famous charities and acquire publics’ trust to raise enough resource.

    Target Population

    People who don’t have periodically donation habit, but have donation attempt and hope to hunt for trustworthy charities.
